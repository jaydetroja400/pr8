Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a center of excellence in education, innovation, and research, producing globally competent and socially responsible professionals.

Mission

  • To impart high-quality technical education and training.
  • To foster innovation, creativity, and leadership skills.
  • To develop strong industry-institute collaborations.
  • To contribute to sustainable community development.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

PSO 1

Apply engineering fundamentals to solve real-world technical problems effectively.

PSO 2

Design and develop efficient software and hardware systems for societal needs.

PSO 3

Demonstrate teamwork, ethics, and lifelong learning in professional practice.
